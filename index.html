K-Otaku Club

K-OTAKU is a student club at ENSAM Meknès, founded in 2015 by passionate fans of anime, manga, video games, cosplay, and Asian culture. It offers a vibrant and welcoming community where students can connect over shared interests, celebrate Japanese and broader Asian traditions, and take part in exciting activities such as gaming tournaments, creative workshops, cultural events, and interactive experiences. Whether you're a long-time otaku or simply curious, K-OTAKU is the perfect place to explore, express, and enjoy your passions.
